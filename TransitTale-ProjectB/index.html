💻⚙️Project Description:💻⚙️

Welcome to Transit Tale! This project is an exploration of the bustling cities of New York and Shanghai through the lens of an international student at an American Univeristy. Dive into the vibrant streets and diverse cultures of these two iconic metropolises. Experience the rhythm of urban life, from the iconic landmarks of New York to the pictures of the futuristic skyline of Shanghai. Click on the buttons below to explore photo albums capturing the essence of each city. Let's embark on a journey through Transit Tale!
