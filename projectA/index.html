[ HYDROSTONEVORE ]

Scientific Name:

Hydrostonevora aquatica

Discovered by / at:

Discovered in 2024, by scientist and explorer Dr. Evergreen in the caribbean

Family:

The Hydrostonevora falls into the Aquamutidae family. This family reflect the creature's ability to undergo mutations responsive to contact with water

Habitat:

Hydrostonevora thrives in a sunny and warm habitat carachterised by turqoise water and really yellow sand .

Appearance:

Dr. Evergreen, a distinguished scientist and inrepid explorer, make a groundbreaking discovery after a month long exploring around the island. While exploring for new forms of life , he encountered an adult hydrostonevore, resembling a circular slime created that mantained its shaped until it reached the water where it transformed to a solid squared shape.

Key Characteristics:

  • Changes color from red to green when touches the water
  • Changes from a circular form in the land to rectangular shape in the water
  • It loves to eat rocks, and this food gives it the nutrients to grow bigger
